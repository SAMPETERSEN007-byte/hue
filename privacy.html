Privacy Policy

Last updated July 14, 2026

The short version: your selfie is analyzed on your device and never uploaded. HUE has no accounts and no cookies — just anonymous, cookieless counts of which steps get used. We couldn't sell your data if we wanted to — we never receive it.

Your photo

Your selfie is processed entirely in your browser, on your device, the moment you select it. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server. When you leave or refresh the page, it is gone. The share card you can generate is also drawn on your device.

Your quiz answers and result

Your color season, unlock status, and — if you arrived through one of our links — a one-word source tag (like "tt") are saved in your browser's local storage so your report is still there when you come back. This data stays on your device — we never see it. Clearing your browser data removes it (if that locks your paid report, contact us and we'll restore your access).

Payments

The $9 full report is purchased through Stripe, our payment processor. Stripe collects the information needed to process your payment (such as your card details and email address) under Stripe's privacy policy. We never see or store your card number. We can see the receipt email you used at checkout, which we use only to handle support and refunds.

If you buy the report, the checkout URL carries a one-word source tag (like "tt" or "friend") and your season name (like "deep-autumn"), so Stripe can show us which link brought the purchase. It never contains anything that identifies you.

Anonymous usage analytics

We use PostHog, a product analytics service, in cookieless mode to count how HUE is used — pageviews and steps like "quiz started," "selfie analyzed," or "checkout clicked." It runs with no cookies and no stored identifier: nothing persists on your device, so separate visits can't be linked to each other, let alone to you. The analytics see that a selfie was analyzed — never the selfie, never your answers, never your palette. Events are received by PostHog (PostHog's privacy policy) on our behalf.

What we don't do

  • No user accounts and no sign-ups — we don't collect your name or email to use HUE.
  • No ad trackers, no tracking cookies, no cross-site tracking, no session recording.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing of personal information — we don't hold any.

Third-party services

Like most websites, a few infrastructure providers technically receive your IP address when they serve you content:

  • GitHub Pages hosts this site (GitHub's privacy statement).
  • Stripe handles checkout, as described above.
  • PostHog receives the anonymous, cookieless usage events described above.

Children

HUE is not directed at children under 13, and since we collect no personal information through the site itself, none is knowingly collected from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted here with an updated date. A change will never retroactively apply to data we don't have.
